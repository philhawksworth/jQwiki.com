jQwiki

jQwiki is a simple, self contained HTML and Javascript Wiki. It uses jQuery to work its magic.

Stay tuned for more information. Changes and new developments are just around the corner and will be announced both here and on the twitter stream.

You can also see how things are shaping up by taking a look at the code at its home on GitHub.
